Welcome!

This site is a home for some of my projects and writing. Not sure where to start? Try making a masterpiece with Yellow Scream (2024), reading about turning a slide whistle into a Trombone Champ controller, or learning about using parametric CAD software for prototyping and laser cutting.

Professionally, I'm a digital signal processing (DSP) engineer. I started with audio, building tools for audio engineers and musicians at iZotope (and winning an Engineering Emmy in the process!). I then worked with EEG (brain signals) as a founding engineer at Beacon Biosignals. I most recently spent three months at the Recurse Center.
