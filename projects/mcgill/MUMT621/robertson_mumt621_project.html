Audio - "The Mason's Apron"

    The following tracks are all included in the code .zip, above.

    The recording used here is by The Chieftains, on their album "An Irish Evening. Live at the Grand Opera House, Belfast." The musicians are Martin Fay (fiddle), Seán Keane (fiddle), Kevin Conneff (bodhran, vocals), Matt Molloy (flute), Paddy Moloney (pipes, whistle), Derek Bell (harp, tiompan, keyboards). The solo is by Matt Molloy (flute). Listen to additional versions of "Mason's Apron" at www.irishtune.info.

    Note: The O'Neil's transcription is in the key of A; it has been transposed to G for the comparison here, since Matt Molloy plays it in G. Automated key invariance is left for a future exercise!
